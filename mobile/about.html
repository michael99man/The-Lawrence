About The Lawrence

The Lawrence is the weekly newspaper of The Lawrenceville School. It is published during the school year except for the periods of Thanksgiving, winter, and spring breaks, by the students of The Lawrenceville School, 2500 Main Street, Lawrenceville, New Jersey 08648, with offices in room 027 in the Lower Level of the Father's Building.

Writing, photography, graphics, and layout are by the students of The Lawrenceville School. Printing is by Bartash Printing, Inc. Opinions expressed on the Opinions pages do not necessarily reflect the opinion of The Lawrenceville School or The Lawrence. The Lawrence reserves the right to edit letters to the editor and opinions.

Letters to the editor should be mailed to the address above with C/O David Figueroa-Ortiz or emailed to lvillelawrence@gmail.com.
